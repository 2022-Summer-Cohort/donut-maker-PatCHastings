• About Fred the Baker

    • Fred the Baker discovered an anomaly in the fabric of space-time when he accidentally dumped donut batter on the particle collider he had built underneath his donut shop. Now.. he has inadvertently created a wormhole through his donut maker, allowing an infinite amount of donuts to be produced. However, these donuts from another dimension have their own laws of physics. It is up to you to help him save the planet..
